Photography

“The camera is an instrument that teaches people how to see without a camera.”
-Dorothea Lange

For reference, I knew absolutely nothing about photography before the start of October. So give me a break I've been doing this for under 20 days. Enjoy.
Also, it doesnt take a pro photogropher or creative genius to point a good camera at something and take a picture on auto mode. If there's anything that I've leaned in the past 20 days, it's not the quality of the picture, but the ability to find the right shot that seperates good photographers from just another person with a camera. I'm still just another person with a camera because all I really do is photograph my pets and other random things around my room, there is so much pontential and it's everywhere but I just too inexperienced to see it, and that okay, there is only room for improvement in the future.
